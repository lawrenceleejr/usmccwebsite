Working groups are where the collaboration's work actually happens — and where you get
involved. Each spans multiple institutions, meets regularly, and welcomes new
contributors, including students and newcomers. Find the area closest to your interests
below and reach out; we'll connect you with the group and its meetings.

## International coordination

We work closely with the [International Muon Collider Collaboration (IMCC)](/otherorgs/imcc/)
and partners at CERN, RAL, INFN, and DESY. Much of the technical work is shared across the
Atlantic, and international meetings are open to newcomers.
