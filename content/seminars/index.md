The collaboration hosts a regular seminar series and keeps a growing library of recorded
talks. Everyone is welcome — you don't need to be a member to attend, and newcomers are
always encouraged to drop in.

## Worlds Collide seminar series

**_Worlds Collide: Interdisciplinary Considerations for a Future Muon Collider_** runs on
**Wednesdays at 2:00 PM Eastern**. The series brings in voices from across — and beyond —
particle physics to explore what building a muon collider really involves, from
accelerator practicalities to how we communicate big ideas.
